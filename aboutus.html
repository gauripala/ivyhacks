About Us

Our Mission

As of right now, there is no current easy, efficient online platform that helps patients in need of organ or blood donors. Hope Funds aims to be a life-saving platform that supports patients in need of a blood or organ donation by matching patients with donors that are a perfect fit for them. With the help of Hope Funds, patients of all demographics and backgrounds (such as families with low incomes and/or without easy access to a hospital) will be supported throughout their journey to recovery.

Who We Are

Hope Funds was created as a product of IvyHacks, a remote three-day virtual hackathon. It was founded by four students passionate about increasing the availability of healthcare to all individuals. Read more about them below!
